ABOUT ME

I’m Emran Hossain, a social entrepreneur, author, and changemaker dedicated to innovation, education, and community empowerment. Through my ventures, my work spans diverse fields, including technology, the environment, literacy, agriculture, and community upliftment. Recognized with awards like Best Youth Organizer (2021) and Top Scout (2018), I believe in turning vision into action. Join me in exploring lots of initiatives designed to inspire, educate, and most importantly make a difference in society.
